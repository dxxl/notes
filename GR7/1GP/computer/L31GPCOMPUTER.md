#Document Organizers

1. Heading Tag
- specifies the heading in a document
ex. <hn>idk anymore</hn>, or <h>the existence coz we can </h>
- the lower the number, the bigger the words
- can have attributes
ex. <h align="center">why are you looking at me? </h>

2. Paragraph Tag
- inserts a blank space
